Journey to the West

The Three Strikes Against the White Bone Demon is a storyline from Journey to the West.
This story focuses on the struggle between the three monks, Sun Wukong, Porky Pig and Sha Wujing,
who are fetching scriptures, and the White Bone Demon.

As the story begins, the four members of the Longevity Monk and his disciples are on their way
to the West to fetch scriptures when they come across a quiet temple. The Tang Sanzang decides to
stay in the temple for a while, but there is a beautiful female goblin named White Bone Goblin in
the temple who intends to hide her identity and secretly eat the Tang Sanzang in order to gain immortality.

Sensing the White Bone Ghost's plot, Sun Wukong manages to expose the White Bone Ghost's true nature
and puts up a series of fights. The White Bone Spirit is very fickle and constantly tries to confuse the
Longevity Monk and his disciples, but is ultimately defeated by the combined efforts of the Monkey King and
his three other disciples.
